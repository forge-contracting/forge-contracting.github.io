Our Story

Forge Contracting is a certified, insured and licensed general contracting company specializing in commercial steel frame and drywall throughout the GTA and Ontario. At FORGE we believe in constantly adapting and growing our young company. We demonstrate thorough knowledge in modern construction building techniques and logistics. Forge Contracting delivers a project on budget and schedule while creating a pleasant atmosphere giving our clients the ability to creatively design worry free. Through years of experience and networking Forge employs a team of educated, experienced and professional trades able to deliver a multitude of projects.
